Our Shop has thousands of products and accents for home and garden, covering every aspect of gardening. We are continually searching to find new merchandise and ideas to help make your garden a special place. We hope that your tour through our catalog will be rewarding for both of us and look forward to serving you.

We do not keep inventory on the webiste. We grow most of the plants at our greenhouse so the inventory is always changing. If the order button is not on the item, we no longer have it available. If something in your order is not available, we will not notify you. We will ship what is available at the time of the order and only charge you for what was shipped. The shipping costs will also be adjusted.
